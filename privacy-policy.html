Privacy Policy for FlyGen AI

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Muhammad Imran Ali ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FlyGen AI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Flyer Content: Text, images, and other content you input to create flyers (headlines, descriptions, dates, contact information, etc.)
  • Logo Images: Optional logo images you upload for inclusion in your flyers
  • QR Code Data: URLs, contact information, or other data you choose to encode in QR codes

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed and actions taken
  • Crash Reports: Technical information collected if the App crashes, to help us improve stability

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered flyer designs based on your input
  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process your transactions and manage your credits
  • Sync your saved flyers across your devices via iCloud (if enabled)
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenRouter API: We send your flyer content (text prompts) to OpenRouter's AI services to generate images. This data is processed according to OpenRouter's privacy policy.
  • Apple iCloud: If you enable iCloud sync, your saved flyers are stored in your personal iCloud account.
  • Apple App Store: In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your flyer content is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers
  • Generated flyers are stored locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect data transmitted to and from the App
  • API communications are encrypted using HTTPS

Data Retention

  • Flyer generation requests are not stored after processing
  • Locally saved flyers remain on your device until you delete them
  • iCloud-synced data follows Apple's iCloud data retention policies

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the flyers and data stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Remove any saved flyers from the App at any time
  • Export: Save generated flyers to your device's photo library
  • Disable iCloud: Turn off iCloud sync in your device settings

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
